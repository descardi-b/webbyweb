39 Revolutions

a Free Republic! how a myth will maintain itself

Four radical left college students travel to Moscow, Russia for spring break to resurrect Vladimir Lenin and rig the 2016 presidential election.

On the cusp of adulthood and revolution, Ivy Leaguers Eve, Lauren, Sarah, and Max decide to take matters into their own hands -- and look really hot while doing it. A raucous bacchanal of sex, drugs, and socialism, 39 Revolutions explores the trials and tribulations of the worst part of growing up: realizing that you might not be invincible after all.

As such, 39 Revolutions begs the question: are we destined to always want more than we could ever have?
