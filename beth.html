beth

your best and wisest Refuge from all troubles is in your Science

If you can't be the best, then nobody else can.

When long-time boyfriend Danny breaks up with study-oriented Beth, she transfers all that turmoil, rage, and heartache into creating the greatest bio research project that this side of the Passaic river has ever seen.

The one catch? Every other overachiever teen at her elite east coast magnet school is prepped to fight for their lives (literally) over the exact same thing. What follows is a bloody, beastly, and bitchy tale of ambition and betrayal as they all aim to secure the ultimate bag: the Intel International Engineering and Science Fair's $75,000 Grand Prize.

A decidedly 21st century Asian-American Macbeth adaptation.
